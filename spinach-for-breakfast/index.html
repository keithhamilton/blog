Spinach for breakfast

10 April 2015 on food, breakfast

Mmm.

Is there any better breakfast food than buttery, garlicky, tangy spinach, topped with a poached egg?

You could definitely make an argument for bacon, and I might agree, but still. Something about cooked spinach and an egg for breakfast rules.

What doesn't rule, however, is how much spinach cooks down. I hate that. Why do I hate that? Well, mainly, it's that I have to pick a pan in which to cook my spinach. I either have to pick a really big pan in which I can easily turn the spinach, or pick a reasonably-sized pan that will suit the end product, but will make the initial steps tough.

Curse you spinach! You are so good for breakfast, but you have no concern for the conveniences of a typically grumpy morning person.

Time to go make some breakfast. With spinach.

Keith Hamilton
